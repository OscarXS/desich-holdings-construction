Greenhouse Construction Built
for Strength, Climate Control & Year-Round Growth

Desich Holdings Construction specializes in greenhouse installation, repairs, and structural upgrades built to support commercial farmers, nurseries, and agricultural developers. Our systems combine durability, ventilation, and smart environmental design to boost productivity and crop protection.

We also provide custom steel framework solutions that enhance stability, improve climate control, and extend the lifespan of every greenhouse. Whether expanding your growing capacity or upgrading an existing setup, our team ensures precise construction and reliable materials for year-round performance.
