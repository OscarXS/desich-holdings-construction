our greenhouses and steel structures

Building Stronger Futures with
Precision Greenhouses & Steel Structures

At Desich Holdings Construction, we specialise in designing and constructing durable greenhouses and high-performance steel structures made to withstand South Africa’s climate. Our solutions combine engineering expertise with modern building standards to deliver long-lasting, energy-efficient environments for farming, storage, commercial use, and industrial growth.
learn more

Custom Agricultural Greenhouses

Designed for optimal crop growth, our tailored greenhouse systems provide controlled environments that improve yields, reduce climate risk, and enhance year-round production for both small farms and commercial agricultural operations.

Steel Warehouse Buildings

We construct heavy-duty steel warehouses engineered for strength, weather resistance, and long-term reliability. These structures offer flexible layouts, secure storage, and low maintenance — ideal for expanding businesses

Hydroponic & Tunnel Farming Structures

From hydroponic tunnels to climate-smart growing facilities, we build systems that support modern farming methods. Each structure maximizes airflow, temperature control, and efficiency to boost sustainable food production.

Greenhouse Renovations & Upgrades

We refurbish and upgrade existing greenhouses with new coverings, improved ventilation, structural reinforcements, and advanced farming technology—extending lifespan and improving overall efficiency.
