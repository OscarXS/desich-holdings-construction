What We Offer

Trusted Construction
Services for Every Project

Desich Holdings (PTY) LTD Construction delivers dependable building and site
development services, providing quality workmanship and practical solutions for every project.
Concrete Slabs
Strong, level, and long-lasting concrete slabs custom-built for homes, industrial sites, and commercial projects. We ensure accurate measurements, proper reinforcement, and high-quality finishing to create durable foundations that stand the test of time.
Retaining Walls
Engineered retaining walls built to prevent soil movement, support landscaping, and protect your property. Our walls combine structural stability with a clean professional finish, improving both safety and aesthetics.
Water Channels & Drainage
Efficient water channel and stormwater drainage solutions designed to control flow, prevent flooding, and safeguard properties. We build reliable systems that ensure long-term performance through proper grading, alignment, and reinforced channels.
Greenhouse Construction
Complete greenhouse structures built with precision and durability. From concrete bases to framework assembly, we provide robust greenhouse solutions that support farming, plant production, and commercial agricultural environments.
Paving & Surface Works
Neat, level, and professionally installed paving for driveways, walkways, commercial spaces, and residential areas. We use durable materials and precise laying techniques to achieve a clean finish and long-lasting results.
Excavation & Site Preparation
Professional site clearing, trenching, leveling, and ground preparation to ensure every project begins on a stable foundation. We use precise methods to prepare land safely and efficiently, creating a solid base for construction work that follows.
